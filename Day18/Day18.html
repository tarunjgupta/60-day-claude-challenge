📄 Meeting Summary
The Quarterly Growth Strategy Meeting reviewed Q2 performance across all departments with 7 senior leaders in attendance — CEO, CFO, CTO, VP Marketing, Head of Sales, Product Director, and Customer Success Lead. The team discussed revenue performance, noting Q2 revenue growth of 12% against a target of 18%, leaving a 6 percentage point gap. 9 of 14 expected enterprise deals were closed, with 5 slipping into Q3. On the positive side, website traffic surged 34% QoQ and customer retention held strong at 94%. The meeting identified critical priorities: recovering slipped enterprise deals, resolving dashboard reporting performance issues impacting both customer retention and new sales, and balancing engineering capacity between website redesign and the planned August analytics dashboard release.
