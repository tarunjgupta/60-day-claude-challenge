Fuel-by-Fuel Breakdown

CNG

  • ✅ Cheapest combustion fuel — ₹3.32/km
  • ✅ Lower CO₂ than petrol & diesel
  • ❌ Refuel takes longer (~8 min)
  • ❌ Thin station network on highways
🚗 Best for: dense city, stop-start driving

Diesel

  • ✅ Strong mileage at 19.6 km/l for highway runs
  • ✅ Quick 5-min refuel, same as petrol
  • ❌ Highest maintenance/km — ₹1.00/km
  • ❌ Highest CO₂/km of all 5 fuels
🚗 Best for: mixed city + highway, high monthly km — fits your Punch

E85 (Flex-Fuel)

  • ✅ Lowest CO₂/km of every fuel — 0.070 kg/km
  • ✅ Fast 5-min refuel like petrol/diesel
  • ❌ Costliest to run — ₹6.37/km despite cheaper pump price
  • ❌ Weakest mileage among combustion fuels
🚗 Best for: eco-first drivers where E85 is heavily subsidised

Electric (EV)

  • ✅ Cheapest running cost by far — ₹1.75/km
  • ✅ Lowest maintenance — ₹0.23/km
  • ❌ 45-min recharge vs 5–8 min for liquid/gas
  • ❌ Shortest range-equivalent per charge
🚗 Best for: city commuters with home/office charging

Petrol (E20)

  • ✅ Lower CO₂/km than diesel
  • ✅ Widest refuel availability, 5-min fill
  • ❌ Second-priciest fuel — ₹6.15/km
  • ❌ Highest fuel price/unit — ₹100
🚗 Best for: mixed driving without diesel/CNG access
