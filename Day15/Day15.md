## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🇮🇳 India’s Progress

* Achieved **E20 blending in 2025** (ahead of schedule)
* ₹1.7 lakh crore forex savings
* ₹1.5 lakh crore paid to farmers

---

### 🚗 Vehicle Impact

* E20 → ~5–10% mileage drop
* E100 → ~25–30% drop
* Older vehicles face compatibility risks

---

### 💰 Cost Reality

* Ethanol is cheaper per litre
* BUT lower mileage cancels benefits

👉 Cost per km ≈ Same or slightly higher

---

### 🌱 Environmental Impact

* E20 reduces CO₂ by ~15–20%
* E100 reduces CO₂ by ~50–60%
* BUT:

  * High water usage
  * Food vs fuel concerns

---

### ⚠️ Consumer Sentiment

* 50% users report mileage drop
* 30% report higher maintenance
* 43% may delay buying petrol vehicles

---

## 📊 Final Verdict

### ✅ Positive

* Reduces oil imports
* Supports farmers
* Improves energy security

### ❌ Challenges

* Mileage reduction
* Water consumption
* Engine compatibility issues

---

## 🔮 Future Outlook

* E20 → Current standard
* E25–E30 → Likely next step
* E100 → Not feasible before 2035+

👉 Ethanol = **Transition Fuel**, not final solution
