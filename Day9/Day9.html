⚠️ Educational Disclaimer

NutriScope is an educational tool designed for informational purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional medical or nutritional advice.

The nutritional values, recommendations, and risk assessments provided are based on general guidelines and may not be accurate for your specific health conditions, allergies, medications, or physiological needs.

  • Always consult a certified dietitian or healthcare provider before making significant dietary changes.
  • Nutritional values are approximate and may vary based on food preparation, brand, and sourcing.
  • This tool does not account for medical conditions such as diabetes, kidney disease, food allergies, or pregnancy.
  • Risk assessments are generalized and should not be used for self-diagnosis.
  • The meal planner generates suggestions based on general nutritional guidelines — individual needs may differ.

Nutrition Data Sources
USDA FoodData Central
U.S. Department of Agriculture nutrient database — primary source for caloric and macronutrient data.
Indian Food Composition Tables (IFCT 2017)
National Institute of Nutrition, India — reference for Indian food items like dal, roti, poha, idli, dosa.
Dietary Reference Intakes (DRI)
National Academies of Sciences — basis for daily recommended intake values for vitamins and minerals.
WHO/FAO Guidelines
World Health Organization and Food & Agriculture Organization — international dietary guidelines and safe upper limits.
Mifflin-St Jeor Equation
Used to estimate Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), widely considered the most accurate predictive equation for healthy adults.
ICMR-NIN RDA (2020)
Indian Council of Medical Research — recommended dietary allowances for the Indian population.
